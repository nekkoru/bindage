Click on the key, then choose the gun you want assigned to that key. Click "Add to bindfile" to commit the buy script.

After you have a buy script you're happy with, copy and paste it into your config.cfg. It's located in steamapps/common/Counter-Strike Global Offensive/csgo/cfg/autoexec.cfg. Just paste it at the end of all the default binds.

To prevent any unnecessary bug reports: "buy ak47" buys an AK if you're playing on T side and an M4 on CT. Same goes for other guns.
